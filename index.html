Revolution 5.0

The Revolution 5.0 comes standard with top shelf components such as a 203mm travel Rockshox Boxxer RC dual crown fork, Vivid R2C Coil shock providing over 9" of frame travel, and the highly regarded Schlumpf High Speed Drive that allows for a 85T equivalent front chainring with the click of your heel.

About

The Revolution is hand welded and 100% built here in the United States. It is designed to be the fastest, most nimble, lightest weight and most powerful downhill electric bike available anywhere. It all started with a handbuilt 6061-T6 Aluminum frame that is hand welded one at a time by a highly esteemed industry professional with over 30 years of TIG welding experience. . .
